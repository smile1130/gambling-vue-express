How To Register BIGMUMBAI Account


Q: How do I register an account?
A: To register an account, please follow the steps below:
1. Please use this link to enter registration menu https://bigmumbai1.com/#/register
2. Please enter the phone number. The number you entered must be correct and completed. The phone number is still active.
3. Set your own login password; the password must be at least 8 digits and contain letters and numbers. You can't enter the symbol @*# at the front or back of the password you create.
4. Confirm the login password.
5. Enter the invitation code.
6. Click I have read and agree [Privacy Agreement].
7. Click on Register.
