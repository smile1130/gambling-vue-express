1.Buying rules:
A blockchain algorithm is utilized to match buyers and sellers.
The buyer transfers funds to the seller via UPI, and before confirming the receipt and ensuring the correct amount, the seller needs to verify that the funds have arrived in their UPI account.
Once confirmed, the seller can proceed to confirm the transaction.
After the transaction is confirmed, the system will automatically release the ARB to the buyer, along with the applicable reward.
