Deposit Not Receive
Q: How i can submit issue about deposit not receive on self-service center BIGMUMBAI?
A: To submit deposit not receive on self-service center BIGMUMBAI, please follow this step
1. You need to use this link https://www.bigmumbaics.com
2. After you enter the link then close the notification
3. Select issue and choose Deposit not receive
4. Fill your BIGMUMBAI ID account
5. Fill deposit amount you transfer
6. Fill UTR number
7. Fill Receiver UPI ID
8. Attach the deposit receipt proof clear and detail
9. Submit issue

STATUS ISSUE
Q: How can I check the status of the issuance deposit not received at the self-service center BIGMUMBAI?
A: You can check the status of the problem by clicking View Problem Status, then fill in your BIGMUMBAI ID account and select the problem of deposit not received. Finally, click Search, and your problem status will be displayed.
